Timing windows

New sessions snapshot these values; a running test is never changed halfway through.

Late-tap grace ms

How long the first person's tap waits for their partner to tap before the cohort starts. Bigger = more forgiving of human timing gaps.

Recommended 5000–8000 ms · allowed 2000–15000

Acoustic exchange window ms

How long the phones emit their coded ultrasonic chirp and keep listening. Bigger = more chances to hear each other, but a longer ceremony.

Recommended 5000–8000 ms · allowed 2400–12000

Bump match slop ms

After both phones prove they heard each other, how far apart their two bumps may be in time and still count as one bump. Bigger = easier to match, but looser.

Recommended 2500–5000 ms · allowed 500–10000
